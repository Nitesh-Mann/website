Google Ads accounts can be suspended for a variety of reasons, including policy violations, suspicious payment activity, circumventing Google's systems, and issues with unacceptable business practices. Our team will help you identify the exact cause and guide you through the recovery process.

The recovery process can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on the complexity of the suspension and Google's response time. We ensure that all appeals and actions are submitted promptly to speed up the process.

While we cannot guarantee 100% reinstatement, our expertise and proven success rate significantly improve the chances of getting your account back. We will work diligently to resolve the suspension and optimize your account to avoid future issues.

We offer personalized attention, in-depth knowledge of Google Ads policies, and a comprehensive approach that includes account review, appeal submission, and campaign optimization. Our goal is to not only get your account reinstated but also improve its performance for long-term success.

Our pricing is transparent and depends on the complexity of your case. We offer different packages starting from account reviews to full recovery services. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific needs.
